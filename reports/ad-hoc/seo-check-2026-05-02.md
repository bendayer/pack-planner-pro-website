- Broad terms like `dog walking software`, `dog walking software uk`, and `dog walker software` are still ranking around positions 52 to 70.

2. The scheduling opportunity is split between blog and landing page.
- The blog is near page 1 for scheduling-style queries.
- The landing page is weaker at average position 18.96.
- The blog should push more authority and clicks into `/dog-walking-scheduling-software`.

3. The no-monthly-fee page is not earning clicks yet.
- 19 impressions, average position 10.21, 0 clicks.
- This is exactly the kind of page where snippet and above-the-fold clarity can matter.

4. `pet-care-software` is visible but too broad.
- 39 impressions at position 55.03 suggests Google sees it, but not as a strong answer.

5. Measurement still cannot prove commercial outcomes.
- GA4 shows organic sessions, but no usable purchase/add-to-cart data in this export.
