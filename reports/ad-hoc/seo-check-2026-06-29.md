## AI / Zero-Click Watch

This export has a mild AI/zero-click watch pattern:

- impressions are rising strongly
- CTR is falling from April to June
- informational and planning/invoicing pages are the current visibility winners
- broad commercial queries are visible but too low to earn clicks

Do not assume AI impact from this alone. Treat it as a proxy signal and focus on better titles, concise direct-answer blocks, stronger FAQs, and internal links from informational pages to the best commercial pages.
