## 1. How to organise your dog walking schedule

### URL
- `/blog/how-to-organise-your-dog-walking-schedule`

### Primary keyword
- dog walking schedule organiser

### Search intent
- informational with commercial undercurrent

### Meta title draft
- How to Organise Your Dog Walking Schedule | Pack Planner Pro

### Meta description draft
- Learn how to organise your dog walking schedule properly. A practical UK guide to planning walks, reducing admin, and staying on top of clients.

### Opening answer capsule
- Organising your dog walking schedule means having one clear system for your weekly walks, client notes, dog details, and last-minute changes. For a small UK dog walking business, the best setup is one that lets you see the whole week at a glance and update it quickly when real life gets in the way.

### Jennie angle
- Start with Jennie's paper diary and notebook stage.
- Bring in the reality of juggling multiple clients, two children, cubs, and school runs.
- Explain that the problem was not laziness or disorganisation. The problem was that the system stopped matching the pace of the business.

### H2 outline
- `Why dog walking schedules get messy so quickly`
- `What stops working when you rely on your head, a notebook, or a paper diary`
- `What a proper weekly dog walking schedule needs to show`
- `How to keep on top of cancellations, changes, and client notes`
- `When it is time to move from a paper system to software`

### Scannable section idea
- checklist:
  - all walks visible by day
  - dogs matched to the right client and time slot
  - changes recorded in one place
  - emergency details easy to find
  - schedule easy to update when plans change

### Soft PPP mention
- If your diary, notes, and memory are all doing different jobs, that is usually the point where a proper planner starts to make life easier. Pack Planner Pro was built for that exact stage of business.

### FAQ ideas
- How do professional dog walkers organise their schedule?
- Should I use a diary, spreadsheet, or software for dog walking?
- Can I manage my dog walking schedule on my phone?
- What is the best way to handle last-minute changes?
