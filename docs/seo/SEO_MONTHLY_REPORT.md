## Technical And Content Read

The repo plus GA4 plus Search Console point to six main priorities.

### 1. Contact route consolidation
- both `/contact.html` and `/contact` appear in Search Console
- this must be normalised across canonical, OG URL, schema, sitemap, and internal links

### 2. Homepage and broad commercial page focus
- the homepage and `dog-walking-software-uk` are the current centres of gravity
- those should be the first pages sharpened for both SEO and conversion

### 3. Snippet and CTR work on near-page-1 pages
- `dog-walking-software-no-monthly-fee` has average position `5.25` with `0` clicks
- `dog-walking-software-for-sole-traders` has average position `13.67` with `0` clicks
- these pages should get title, description, and above-the-fold refinement now

### 4. Social image and metadata cleanup
- broken OG image references still need fixing
- metadata consistency still matters because impressions are now real

### 5. Blog and legacy-content decision
- `/blog` and three blog article routes are still receiving impressions
- decide whether those routes are strategic assets or redirect targets

### 6. Conversion measurement
- conversion tracking remains the biggest reporting gap
- without key events, SEO wins cannot be tied to business outcomes
